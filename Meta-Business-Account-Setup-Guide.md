# How to Set Up Your Meta Business Account (for WhatsApp)

Welcome! To use WhatsApp for your business, we first need to set up a **free Meta (Facebook) Business account** in **your** name. This account holds your WhatsApp number and your billing — it stays fully owned by you.

This guide is written so **anyone can follow it**, step by step. It takes about **15–20 minutes**. You only do this **once**.

> 👉 You create the account; **we (the Desire Div team) do all the technical WhatsApp setup for you** after you give us access (Step 5).

---

## ✅ Before you start — keep these ready

- [ ] A **Facebook login** (email/phone + password). *Don't have one? Step 1 shows you how — it's free and your personal profile stays private.*
- [ ] Your **business details**: business name, website (if any), address, and a **logo/photo**.
- [ ] A **mobile number for WhatsApp** — see the important note below. ⬇️
- [ ] A **debit/credit card** (Meta charges a small amount per message; this card is yours and stays in your account).
- [ ] About **15–20 minutes**.

> ⚠️ **Very important about the WhatsApp number:**
> The phone number you want to use for business WhatsApp **must NOT already be active on the normal WhatsApp app**.
> - If you want to use a number that currently has WhatsApp on it, you must **delete WhatsApp from that number first** (Open WhatsApp → Settings → Account → Delete my account), **or**
> - Use a **fresh/new number** that has never had WhatsApp.
> The number must be able to **receive an SMS or a phone call** (we'll send a 6-digit code to confirm it).

---

## Step 1 — Create a Facebook account (skip if you already have one)

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Safari, etc.) and go to **https://www.facebook.com**
2. Click **"Create new account"**.
3. Fill in your name, mobile number or email, password, date of birth → click **Sign Up**.
4. Confirm the code Facebook sends to your email/phone.

✅ That's it — you now have a Facebook login. *(You don't need to post anything; this is just your secure login.)*

---

## Step 2 — Create your Meta Business account

1. In your browser, go to **https://business.facebook.com**
2. If asked, **log in** with the Facebook account from Step 1.
3. Click the blue **"Create account"** button (top right).
4. A box will pop up. Fill in:
   - **Business name** → your business name (this is what shows on bills).
   - **Your name** → your full name.
   - **Business email** → an email **you can check now** (a code will be sent here).
5. Click **Submit** / **Next**.
6. **Check your email inbox** for a message from Meta/Facebook → open it → click **"Confirm"** (or the confirmation link).

✅ Your Meta Business account is now created. 🎉

---

## Step 3 — Add your business details

1. You should now be inside **Business Settings** (if not, click the **gear ⚙️ / "Settings"** icon, then **"Business settings"**).
2. On the left, click **"Business info"**.
3. Fill in your **address**, **website** (if any), and other details, then **Save**.

*(This helps Meta approve your account and raises your sending limits later.)*

---

## Step 4 — Give us (Desire Div) access to set up WhatsApp

This is the step that lets **us do the technical work for you**.

1. Still in **Business Settings**, on the left side click **"Users" → "People"**.
2. Click the blue **"Add" / "Invite People"** button.
3. In the email box, type the email we gave you:

   > **Our email to add:** ____________________________
   > *(We will send you this email address — type it exactly.)*

4. For the role, choose **"Full control" / "Admin"** (so we can set everything up).
5. Click **Next / Send invite**.

✅ Done. We'll get the invite and accept it. *(You can remove our access anytime later — you stay the owner.)*

---

## Step 5 — Be ready for the WhatsApp number + payment

Once we have access, **we'll do the WhatsApp setup** (create your WhatsApp Business account, connect your number, etc.). During this, we'll need you to:

1. **Tell us the phone number** you want to use for business WhatsApp (remember the rule in the "Before you start" note ⬆️).
2. **Share the 6-digit code** that Meta sends to that number (by SMS or call) so we can confirm it's yours. *(We'll be on a call or chat with you for this — it takes 1 minute.)*
3. **Add your payment card** — either you add it, or we add it together on a quick screen-share. Meta bills **this card** directly for your messages (small per-message cost).

That's everything you need to do. 🙌

---

## (Optional but recommended) Step 6 — Business verification

To send to **more people per day** and show a verified business name, Meta may ask you to **verify your business** (upload a document like a business registration or utility bill).

- You'll see a **"Start verification"** prompt in Business Settings → **"Security Center"** or **"Business info"**.
- It's free. We'll guide you if/when it's needed. You can start using WhatsApp before this is done.

## ❓ Common questions

**Q: Is this account mine or yours?**
It's **100% yours.** You own the business account, the WhatsApp number, and the billing. We're just added as a helper to set it up, and you can remove us anytime.

**Q: Will my personal Facebook be visible to customers?**
No. Your personal profile stays private — this is only used as your secure login.

**Q: Who pays for the messages?**
You do — Meta charges **your** card directly, at their standard rates. We charge you only our agreed **monthly service fee** (separately).

**Q: Can I use my existing WhatsApp number?**
Only if you **delete WhatsApp from that number first** (see the note at the top). Otherwise, use a new number. Many businesses use a **separate number** for this so their personal WhatsApp keeps working.

**Q: I'm stuck on a step.**
No problem — send us a screenshot of where you are and we'll guide you. We can also do most of this together on a quick call/screen-share.
